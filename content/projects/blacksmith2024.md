### What I Did

- Contributed to the development of the game for **Game Jam Plus 24/25 Indonesia** as part of a small, cross-functional team (*The Bois Project*).
- Collaborated using **Git-based version control**, supporting parallel development and stable feature integration.
- Architected and implemented core gameplay systems, including **time management**, **crafting**, **inventory management**, and **customer/order handling**.
- Actively participated in **game design and iteration**, refining the core gameplay loop, pacing, and player experience.
- Co-created the project **pitch deck**, collaborating with the team to clearly communicate the game vision, mechanics, and market positioning.
